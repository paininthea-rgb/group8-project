Typography

This section describes the fonts that we use in our dashboard, different typography elements, their sizing and styling.

Font Family

Syncopate
Montserrat
Noto

In our dashboard we use 3 Fonts - Syncopate, Monstserrat and Noto. The first one, being more stylish and inspiring class is used titles. For content and for the purpose of readability, we use Montserrat font. For data representation and charts, we use noto, a serif font that pairs well with Montserrat.
